Algan Transport and Logistics

Our Services

ROAD

As ALGAN INTERMODAL, we arrange mutual departures every day of the week between Turkey and European countries.

SEA

Algan Intermodal offers FCL and LCL services thanks to our sectorial and industrial experience and globally well-experienced staff focused on perfectionism .

AIR

Thanks to our professional agent network, Algan Intermodal carries air transportation services to each and every spot throughout the world.

MINIVAN

In addition to your general cargo, we also offer guaranteed door-to-door delivery for hazardous and heat-controlled loads by Minivans in no later than 48 hours

ADR TRANSPORT

Algan Intermodal carries out the logistics of dangerous goods, which are the main needs of many important sectors, in accordance with the ADR Convention and all international legislations.

WAREHOUSING

We offer warehouse services for many of your products, such as dry cargo, parcel and racked textiles in our warehouses located in many parts of Europe.
