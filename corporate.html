RESPECT TO THE ENVIRONMENT

We are also aware of our responsibilities to the environment as a logistics company. We strive to use environmentally friendly methods in all of our logistics operations and take care to keep our carbon footprint to a minimum. We are constantly strengthening the infrastructure required for intermodal transportation, which is one of the eco-friendliest transportation methods, expanding our equipment and concentrating on carrying out our transports using this method.
