Automotive Spare Parts

Automotive transportation specialists are well-experienced individuals who clearly are aware of importance and challenges of such a enormous and global industry. In this order, the slightest failure in the supply chain can be a serious problem and a significant material loss for the manufacturers. Algan Intermodal produces sector-oriented solutions with our professional team to prevent disruption of the supply chain in our logistics operations for the automotive sector, which has an important share in Turkey’s exports.

Our main services in automotive industry:

  • Supply chain control towers
  • Customs procedures
  • Load shipment network planning
  • Transportation options
  • Cross-docking
  • IT systems management

Food Industy

Logistics of food products requires serious attention and expertise as it directly affects human health. As food play a highly-important role in our lives, we must understand and improve the way of growth, process and handle. Generally, food industry is comprised of a complicated network of activities related to supply, consumption and catering. The products must be transported at a specified temperature and this temperature should never change during the logistics process. In addition, care must be taken to ensure maximum hygiene.

Our main services in food industry:

  • Food warehouse containers
  • Reefer and dry containers
  • Intermodal transportation solutions
  • Warehousing and domestic distribution

Textile Industry

Logistics operations for the textile and garment sector, which is one of the areas we provide the most intensive service since our establishment, require serious experience and expertise. The most important feature that distinguishes the textile sector from others is its speed of transformation. Algan Intermodal cooperates with fashion-specialized teams focused on planning, implementation and management of appropriate supply chain solutions for fast-moving garments industry. Shaped according to your needs, we offer high quality services for end-to-end rapid supply for garments.

Our offers for textile industry:

  • Collections/services
  • Consolidation
  • Document check/preparation
  • Quantities and limited quality assurance
  • Loading optimization and specialized equipment
  • GOH ( Garment on Hanger ) storage, usage and special equipment

Mining , Energy & Construction

In recent years, significant investments have been made both in Turkey and in Europe, especially in important areas such as mining , energy and construction. The logistics of out-of-gauge products, which are often needed in these projects, requires considerable expertise and experience. It is not possible to transport these loads, which are in very large forms in terms of weight or volume, by common transportation methods. Therefore, it requires an important equipment pool and infrastructure investment. Algan Intermodal makes a difference with our expert and experienced team in project transport besides our transportation methods specific for heavy industry products.

Chemical & Petro-Chemistry

Chemistry is life… Most of the industries are based on the products produced by chemistry industry. Beside liquid or solid form, chemicals form the basis of food, medicaments and any and all daily products. Chemicals industry require attention for 24/7. We very well know that chemistry industry does not tolerate any fault. We are aware of the demands for your loads, such as transparency, safety, DiT ( Delivery in Time ) , cost-efficient solutions, environmental regulations etc.. Algan Intermodal delivers all of your chemical products to your destination safely with our experienced staff in the chemical industry, whether or not they are classified as dangerous goods.

Electronic Industry

With the placement of technology in the centre of our lives, electronic products are needed in almost every field. In the electronics sector, which is under pressure in terms of timing and cost, it is expected to provide cost-effective, fast and effective solutions for manufacturers and suppliers.As ISD Logistics, we make a difference with our special solutions for the electronics sector. We carry out the logistics processes of these products, which are mostly delicate, with great care and ensure maximum safety. In addition, we carry out the transportation process from the beginning to the end in a transparent manner and we offer our customers the opportunity to follow up at every stage.
