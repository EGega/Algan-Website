INTERMODAL (FCL & LCL)

Intermodal Transportation is the method, where at least two diverse transportation modes are utilized without replacing the container. Being the most brand new innovator intermodal transportation company in Turkey, we ensure our customers decrease their costs and offer the most economical and environment-friendly solution through optimum transit periods.
The basic combinations we use are as follows;
Road + RO-RO + Rail
Road + Rail
The model to be used is determined mainly in terms of the following: final destination of the load, urgency, seasonal and economical factors.
FOR EXAMPLE: Road transportation from Istanbul to Amsterdam , distance is almost 270 kms. Intermodal method for the same route, descreases road for only 150 kms. As a result of this, fossil fuel consumption as well as carbon emissions are reduced.

PROJECT TRANSPORT

Project loads also known as “ Out of Gauge” , are distinguished from other loads by their volume and weight. The transportation of these loads, which are extremely high, wide and heavy, is a special area of expertise. It also requires many special methods, tools and equipment. Algan Intermodal plan the processes of project transport from beginning to end with great care and make a difference with our expert team and experience. We have the privilege of cooperating with state companies in Turkey and Europe in different Project Loadings .

MINIVAN

In addition to your general cargo, we also offer guaranteed door-to-door delivery for hazardous and heat-controlled loads by Minivans in no later than 48 hours. Guaranteed delivery in 48 hours to Europe. As one of the fastest and most practical transportation methods, Minivan transport is more convenient than air transportation with its cost advantage; road transport with its faster delivery time. Furthermore, it is not affected by the transfer of the load to a different vehicle, delays in landing and take-off of the aeroplanes and the difficulties caused by the lack of airports everywhere.
